Everyone wants to talk about the founding. Nobody warns you about the Tuesday
in month four when two interns drop out, a partner startup goes quiet, and you
have a chemistry test in the morning.

## The unglamorous middle

The middle is where the actual learning lives. Founding is a feeling;
sustaining is a skill. I learned to write the email I was dreading first, to
ask for help before things broke, and to treat other people's time as the
scarcest resource I was managing.

## What changed in me

I am more patient than I was, and less impressed by beginnings. The work that
matters is almost always the work that is boring to describe.
