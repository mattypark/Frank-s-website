The first time a price moved on news I had read minutes earlier, I understood
something that no textbook had managed to teach me: a market is a sentence the
whole world is writing at once.

## Prices are arguments

Every quote is a claim — that this is what a thing is worth, right now, to
someone willing to act. Read enough of them in a row and you are no longer
looking at numbers. You are reading a paragraph about fear, patience, and
belief.

This is the part of finance I love, and the part I think gets lost when it is
taught as arithmetic.
